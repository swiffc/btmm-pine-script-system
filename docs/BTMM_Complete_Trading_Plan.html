🔄 TYPE 1: M/W Patterns Above/Below Asian Session (Safety Trade)

✅ Type 1a: Full 13 EMA Rules 📈

📝 Rules:

  • Both legs strictly respect the 13 EMA
  • No break or violation allowed
  • Apex forms with clean rejection candlestick pattern
  • Works on ALL timeframes

📍 Optimal Locations:

  • Session HOD/LOD
  • PSH/PSL, PDH/PDL, PWH/PWL, PMH/PML, PYH/PYL
  • 5-day averaged ADR levels

🎯 Use Case:

Ideal in trending markets with clear structure

✅ Type 1b: 13 EMA Tap 🔄

📝 Rules:

  • First leg taps the 13 EMA but doesn't break it
  • Second leg forms rejection candlestick pattern
  • Examples: Morning Star, Railroad Tracks, COW
  • Triggers on every timeframe when visible

📍 Optimal Locations:

  • Brinks Timing: 9:45 PM, 3:45 AM, 9:45 AM EST
  • London/NY session extremes
  • Key reversal levels: PSH/PSL, PDH/PDL, PWH/PWL

✅ Type 1c: Leg Does Not Touch 13 EMA 📊

📝 Rules:

  • First leg remains entirely above (buy) or below (sell) 13 EMA
  • Second leg closes back inside 13 EMA
  • Confirms with candlestick rejection (Railroad Tracks, COW)
  • Valid on all timeframes when pattern is clear

📍 Optimal Locations:

  • Near session HOD/LOD
  • Liquidity sweeps before reversal

⚡ 5. OTE SETUPS (OPTIMAL TRADE ENTRY)
🎯 OTE FORMATION: After break above/below PDH, PDL, PWH, PWL, PMH, PML, PYH, PYL, PSH, PSL

📊 Type 1: Standard OTE After Reference Level Break

🎯 Setup Process:

  1. Break Reference Level: Price breaks above/below key level
  2. First Leg: Impulse move away from reference level
  3. OTE Retracement: 62-79% pullback (70.5% optimal)
  4. Second Leg: Continuation from OTE zone

📍 Reference Levels for OTE:

PDH/PDL
PWH/PWL
PMH/PML
PYH/PYL
PSH/PSL

🔄 Type 2: OTE Divergence Setup

🎯 Divergence Criteria:

  • Setup: Price takes out high of first leg BUT closes back below within 3 candles
  • Timeframe: Works on EVERY timeframe (1M, 5M, 15M, 1H, 4H, Daily)
  • Signal: Creates divergence and potential OTE opportunity
  • Psychology: Smart money rejecting higher prices, retail trapped

📝 Execution Steps:

  1. Price breaks above first leg high (liquidity grab)
  2. Price closes below first leg high within 3 candles
  3. New OTE zone created from divergence high
  4. Look for lower timeframe confirmation in OTE
🚀 6. ADVANCED ENTRY PATTERNS

🅼🆆 M&W Patterns - Extended Rules

📝 BUY/SELL Rules:

  • First leg doesn't close above/below 13 but consolidates in range of 8+ candles
  • TDI confirmation: RSI outside band (first leg)
  • Comes back and closes above/below MBL crossing signal line

📍 Found at:

  • HOD or LOD
  • Level 3 reversal areas
  • YH or YL (blue tracer)
  • After SHH or SHL above/below Asian range
  • At EMAs (water, mayo, etc.)
  • After ADR met or exceeded

🦇 Half Batman Pattern

📝 Rules (Type 1&2) - Lev3, 15min/1hr:

  • Outside structure, first leg
  • Price closes above/below 13 and comes back to challenge first leg
  • Price fails to reach first leg by 10 pips
  • Entry trigger: shift candle 10-12 pips
  • Shift candle closes above/below apex (middle of structure)
  • Price closes above/below 13 with TDI confirmation

📊 ID50 / ID50 H1

📝 Rules (Lev1, 15min) / (Lev1&3, 1hr/4hr/Daily):

  • Must have anchor present left
  • 13/50 cross
  • First pullback to 50 (20-25 pips) - quick move back to 50
  • Wait for price to trap at 50 EMA
  • Entry trigger: RR, COW, morning/evening star closing below 13
  • TDI confirmation: RSI/Signal line cross

📈 M&W off 200 EMA

📝 Rules (Mayo/Water) - L2, 15min/1hr/4hr:

  • Same entry rules as M/W patterns
  • Second leg closes above/below 13
  • TDI confirmation required
  • Works on all timeframes when visible
